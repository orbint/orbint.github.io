SeRANIS and Orbint at Space Tech Expo 2025

Nov 18, 2025

Last week, Leonhard Kessler and Daniel Weinzierl from the Universität der Bundeswehr München had the pleasure of presenting SeRANIS – Mission and our university spin-off Orbint at the joint booth during the Space Tech Expo Europe Bremen.

The event was an excellent opportunity to engage with the professional community and share insights into our work on next-generation satellite technologies.

🛰️ SeRANIS 🛰️

SeRANIS (Seamless Radio Access Networks for Internet of Space) is part of the German Armed Forces Center for Digitalization and Technology Research (dtec.bw). dtec.bw is funded by the European Union – NextGenerationEU. The SeRANIS project is being carried out by the SPACE Research Center at the University of the German Armed Forces in Munich (UniBw M).

From: Leonhard Kessler and Anja von Bosse from UniBw M
