Orbint at Rohde & Schwarz International Developers Conference (IDC) 2026

Apr 29, 2026

We had the opportunity to participate in this year's Rohde & Schwarz International Developer Conference (IDC) 2026 in Munich. 🌍🛰️

Presenting Orbint to Rohde & Schwarz's global R&D community was an honour. IDC brings together expertise across AI, software, hardware, and advanced technologies, creating a unique setting for focused technical exchange and forward-looking discussions.

What we appreciated most were the conversations throughout the event. Engaging directly with developers and engineers across the ecosystem led to insightful discussions on how we can jointly build and scale next-generation, highly reliable space-based systems. The depth of expertise and openness we experienced at IDC underline the strong foundation for future collaboration.

Thank you to everyone involved! We look forward to continuing discussions and shaping what comes next, together.
