"Tag der Raumfahrt" in Berlin: Space Cafe Clip

Mar 31, 2025

Exciting news from the "Tag der Raumfahrt" in Berlin! We had the privilege of sitting down with Alexander Schmidt, co-founder of Orbint GmbH, to dive into the future of satellite-based signal intelligence.

Alexander shared Orbint's mission to revolutionize global signal detection and analysis, emphasizing their commitment to delivering precise, actionable insights through advanced technology. He also highlighted the company's roots, with a team boasting extensive experience in signal intelligence and satellite systems engineering from the University of the Bundeswehr Munich.

This conversation underscored the pivotal role of satellite technology in enhancing global communication and security. Orbint's innovative approach is set to make significant waves in the industry.

Enjoy the full interview, where Alexander discusses Orbint's upcoming projects and their vision for the future of space-based intelligence.

From: Torsten Kriening, SpaceWatch.Global TV
