02 // What We Stand For

See Everything.

Orbint's mission is to deliver timely, reliable, and actionable radio-frequency intelligence from space. We design and operate distributed satellite systems that detect, identify, and geolocate RF emitters worldwide in near real time, enabling defence and security customers to maintain situational awareness and make informed decisions across the electromagnetic spectrum.

Decide with Confidence.

Orbint envisions a world in which decision-makers have continuous, trusted insight into the electromagnetic environment, anywhere on Earth and at any time. By building a scalable, software-defined intelligence layer in orbit, we aim to set the standard for space-based RF sensing and to become a cornerstone capability for modern security, defence, and resilience operations.

Sovereign by Design.

Orbint is built from the ground up as a European capability. From our roots at the University of the Bundeswehr Munich to our strategic partnership with Rohde & Schwarz, we are committed to keeping critical intelligence infrastructure in European hands. Defence and security customers retain full sovereignty over their most sensitive data and operational capabilities.
