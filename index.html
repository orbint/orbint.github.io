Technology

Signal intelligence for operators who cannot afford gaps.

Orbint delivers the full stack from space-qualified RF receivers to the analyst interface. No data leaves European infrastructure.

01
Electromagnetic Order of Battle
Create and update a real-time map of radar systems, radio networks, and electronic jamming gear across restricted or hostile zones. Multi-satellite signal processing locates each transmitter with sub-kilometer precision, while onboard analysis identifies its specific modulation, pulse patterns, and timing variations.
02
Maritime surveillance
Ships that go dark by disabling their AIS transponder still radiate. Orbint correlates radar emissions and communications signatures against fingerprint profiles to resolve position to sub-kilometer accuracy, fused with prior tracks and terrain models in near real time.
03
Crisis early warning
A new emitter appearing overnight, a radar shifting frequency, a transmitter changing its pulse rhythm: these are the signals that precede action. Orbint flags the change the moment a satellite pass captures it, before the situation on the ground becomes visible.
04
Spectrum monitoring
Oceanic and polar regions fall outside any terrestrial monitoring network. Orbint covers the full spectrum from UHF through Ka-band globally from LEO, with all collected data archived end-to-end encrypted under European sovereign ground infrastructure and German jurisdiction.
