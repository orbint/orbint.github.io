Privacy Policy

At ORBINT, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require any login or account creation to use our website. However, when you fill out our contact form, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To address your inquiry or message.
  • Email Address: To respond to your message or inquiry.
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding your inquiry, if necessary.
  • Message Content: Any details you provide in the contact form that are necessary to assist with your request.

We do not collect sensitive information such as financial data or health-related information.

2. How We Use Your Information

The personal information you provide through the contact form is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries, messages, or requests.
  • To improve the content and functionality of our website.
  • To ensure the proper functioning and security of our website.

We do not use your information for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information you submit through our website.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is solely used to address your inquiry or message and is not shared with external organizations, except as required by law.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies to collect personal information. However, third-party services (such as Google Analytics) may use cookies to analyze how visitors interact with the website. These third-party cookies are not under our control, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

7. Your Rights

Since we only collect information through the contact form, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information you've submitted.
  • Correct any inaccuracies in the information you've provided.
  • Delete any information we hold about you (within the limitations of our legal obligations).

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact form or at info@orbint.de.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Revised" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us via our contact form.
